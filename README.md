# Google Play Store GTM Analysis

## Project Overview
This project performs a **Go-To-Market (GTM) analysis** on the Google Play Store dataset. The goal is to understand market trends, app performance, and opportunities for launching new apps.

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle – Google Play Store dataset
- Rows: 10,841
- Columns: 13 (App, Category, Rating, Reviews, Size, Installs, Type, Price, Content Rating, Genres, Last Updated, Current Ver, Android Ver)
- Cleaned for missing values, data types, and numeric conversions.

## Analysis Steps
1. **Market Landscape**
   - Number of apps per category
   - Average installs, ratings, and price per category
   - Free vs Paid app distribution
   - Recency of updates

2. **Segmentation Analysis**
   - Popularity tiers (Low, Medium, High, Very High)
   - Price tiers (Free vs Paid)
   - Category and rating segmentation

3. **Insights**
   - Top-performing categories
   - Pricing strategies
   - Segment opportunities
   - Recommendations for app launch strategy

## Tools & Libraries
- Python 3.x
- pandas, numpy
- matplotlib, seaborn
- datetime

## Key Takeaways
- Games, Family, and Education apps dominate downloads.
- Most apps are free; paid apps must focus on unique value.
- High ratings correlate with higher installs — quality matters.
- Frequent updates increase app reliability and user trust.
